# Project 2 — Pivot Tables & Interactive Dashboard

**Skill demonstrated:** Create pivot tables, charts, and dashboards to present key metrics.

## Business question
Leadership needs a one-page, at-a-glance view of retail sales performance
by category, location, and payment method — with the ability to filter by
year and month without touching any formulas.

## What the dashboard shows
- Total revenue and total transactions (KPI cards)
- Revenue by category (bar chart)
- Revenue by location — Online vs In-store (pie chart)
- Monthly revenue trend (line chart)
- Revenue by payment method (bar chart)

## Tools used
PivotTables · PivotCharts · Slicers · Timeline · KPI Cards · Conditional Formatting

## Files
- `raw-data/` — source dataset
- `pivot-dashboard.xlsx` — dashboard workbook
- `screenshots/` — dashboard views
